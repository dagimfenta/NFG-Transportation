About Us

Who We Are

NFG Transportation is a full-service logistics company committed to moving freight safely, on time, and at competitive rates. Built on years of hands-on experience in the trucking industry, we understand the demands of shippers and carriers alike.

Our team combines deep industry knowledge with modern technology to deliver dependable solutions for businesses of every size.

Our Mission

We exist to simplify freight movement. Whether it's a single load or a long-term contract, we treat every shipment as our own — ensuring clear communication, transparent pricing, and reliable delivery from pickup to drop-off.

Safety, integrity, and customer satisfaction drive every decision we make.
